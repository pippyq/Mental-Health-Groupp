Our Services

Individual Counseling

We provide trauma-informed therapy to individuals of all ages. Our approach centers on healing, cultural sensitivity, and personal growth. Sessions are available in English, Spanish, and Arabic.

Domestic Violence Support

Our trained advocates offer crisis intervention, safety planning, shelter coordination, and legal resources for survivors of domestic abuse — including Muslim, immigrant, and LGBTQ+ individuals.

Youth Programs

We support youth through mentoring, group therapy, and after-school wellness activities. All staff are trained to work with trauma, disabilities, and behavioral challenges.

Housing & Resource Help

We help families and individuals access emergency shelter, transitional housing, food, clothing, and public benefits. Services are available to all — regardless of immigration or financial status.
